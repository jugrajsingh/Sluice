## How to sign

Sluice uses **[cla-assistant.io](https://cla-assistant.io/)** to collect CLA signatures. When
you open a pull request, the cla-assistant GitHub App will post a comment asking you to sign.
Click the link, review this document, and authenticate with your GitHub account. Your signature
is recorded once and applies to all future contributions.

If you are unable to sign via cla-assistant, email jugrajskhalsa@gmail.com with your full name,
GitHub username, and the statement: *"I have read the Sluice CLA v1.0 and agree to its terms."*
